It’s safe to say that the exterior look of your home or building is very important. First impressions count! However, many of us do tend to focus more on what is going on inside of our homes or workplaces. Have you ever considered exterior painting and decorating before?

As lovely as your office may be inside, if the exterior is unmaintained, untouched, and ignored, it’s hardly going to inspire people to come inside! It’s not unheard of for people to hire an interior design team to spruce up the outside, too.

More and more people are considering decorating their exterior facades. Of course, designing your exterior the right way will require time, energy, and expertise! Thankfully, The Good Painter has a few ideas to help you get started – before you go ahead and splash too much exterior paint about!

Consider your materials

Sherwin Williams

Little Greene

The first thing that you will want to consider before starting any exterior painting and decorating job is getting suitable materials. No matter where you live, the weather will always affect the exterior of your home and your workplace. You need to make sure that the materials that you are planning on using outside are capable of withstanding the different circumstances that it will face.

It makes sense to look for specialist paint for outdoor surfaces and rough material. For example, you can invest in paint for use on metal as well as wooden posts and frames. Do also consider paint that’s going to withstand threats such a mould growth and UV.

If you’re really unsure about the paint and the tools you’ll need for exterior painting jobs, it pays to speak to London design professionals. A bit more on that a little further down! Outdoor spaces can look fantastic with a little TLC.

Plan ahead with common sense

As is the case when it comes to interior design, when you are planning on undergoing an exterior makeover for your home or workplace, then you need to be prepared for how long the process is going to take.

Your exterior painting will most likely not look perfect overnight, and in order for things to last for as long as possible outside, some groundwork will be necessary. Treating the outdoor materials before painting them, for example, is a must – and this will add to the overall time and effort taken.

Exterior paint jobs do require different preparation and materials from job to job. That is why interior design companies who also specialise in exterior painting and decorating, are very useful in these circumstances.

Consider colour trends!

Charleston Blonde

Jan Baldwin

If you are completely redoing a workplace’s exterior walls, then keeping up with the latest colour trends is an excellent idea! Making sure that your exterior follows current tastes will give your business a modern and sophisticated look. A top London interior design and build design studio will help point you in the right direction.

If your place of work is relatively new, then setting the style standards early on will help bolster your business’ image. It makes sense to get on-trend as soon as possible, and the outside of your building is the best place for this. Any design and build studio experts will agree with us!

If, however, your business has been around for a while, then you will already know that revamping the look of a building from the exterior will make it more likely to catch the eyes of passers-by. Even the most famous companies choose to recreate their looks and change their colour schemes from time to time.

Some of the top colour trends for 2021 are pale greys, blues and greens, as well as darker colours such as bronze, black, navy blue and earthy green. Why not try a mix while keeping on-brand? Ask for help from interior London experts if you’re unsure.

Why hire professional painters and decorators?

Our Team

When it comes to exterior painting and decorating, it is far better to hire professionals than to take it all on yourself. Keeping up with all of the latest exterior trends is one thing, but knowing what materials to use, what processes need to be completed to ensure the longevity of your paint job is completely different!

Hiring professional interior design experts means taking time, effort and expense off your shoulders. That means more time for you to actually run your business, and less time worrying about how it all looks.
