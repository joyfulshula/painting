Our process, explained

With our quick and easy five step process you will always know whats next

  • Initial consultation
  • Planning
  • Preparation and painting
  • Cleaning
  • Handover

Initial consultation

Our friendly, professional team gets in touch with you to discuss your project.

When we are talking to you, we dont just listen to hear, we listen to we understand. We also understand that not everyone is an expert in the world of decorating, thats why we are here after all! We work with you to arrive at the best possible solution thats most suited to your project requirements. We have a good knowledge of the market and are confident to advise you on many subjects, from colour and material specifications to the durability and pricing of different materials.

Planning

We work together with you the plan the project following your requirements

After establishing the exact scope of the project based on the consultation process, we prepare our detailed plans and documents for the works needed, so everything is plain and simple. We establish a timeline that works the best for you then assign and brief in a project team chosen to your specific requirements.

Finally, we purchase all the products, materials, tools and protection needed. Dont worry, these are all covered in your package price already!

Preparation and painting

Are you ready for the big day? We sure are!

Our team arrives on the site with all the required tools and materials. As long as access is organised, you dont even need to be in if you prefer not to. We can pick up and drop off the keys from a neighbour or nearby location thats convenient for you.

We protect all surfaces, built-in and freestanding furniture with maximum care and prepare the areas for decorating. Lets just say, the next part is we are best known for Painting. As experts in the field, we always work with the highest quality products and best practices to ensure consistently outstanding results.

Cleaning

You will only know we have been there from the amazing work we left behind.

Once all the coats have dried and your dedicated project manager have given the final approval to the team, ensuring perfect quality throughout, our team removes all waste materials and thoroughly cleans and tidies the property. We take extra care to return everything just how we found it. We can drop the keys for you at a convenient nearby location.

For larger projects a professional cleaning company can also be provided just ask.

Handover

We will make sure you are 100% happy with the result

Our work isnt over until you are completely satisfied. We follow up to review everything with you and discuss how to maintain the surfaces. We also issue you a detailed digital copy of your finishes schedule for future reference, so you will have it on hand the next time you need it.
